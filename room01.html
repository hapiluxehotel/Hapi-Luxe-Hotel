About This Room

Tropical calm, tailored for slow mornings.

Room 01 is designed in a warm tropical style, blending natural wood tones with soft cream textiles and gold accents. A private balcony opens onto the quieter side of the building, giving you a peaceful spot for morning coffee above the city.

The room's centrepiece bathtub, generous king bed and dedicated workspace make it equally suited to couples on a slow city break or business travellers who want a calm place to return to at the end of the day — all just minutes from Bến Thành Market and District 1's main attractions.
